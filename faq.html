What is the Computer Science Honor Society?

The Computer Science Honor Society (CSHS) is an organization dedicated to promoting excellence in computer science education and fostering a collaborative environment for students passionate about technology.

How can I join the club?

You can join by visiting our Sign Up page and filling out the membership form.

What activities does the club participate in?

Our members engage in various activities, including workshops, hackathons, coding challenges, and outreach events.

What are the benefits of being a member?

As a member, you'll gain access to exclusive resources, mentorship from professionals, and opportunities to work on exciting projects.

Do I need prior experience in computer science to join?

No, prior experience is not required! We welcome students of all skill levels.

Are there any membership fees?

Yes, there are fees to join the Computer Science Honor Society. But the fee won't start until next year.

How can I get involved with club events?

Keep an eye on our Projects page and newsletters for upcoming events.
